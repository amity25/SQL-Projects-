Business Question:

What stock level does each product have?

Query:

SELECT
p.ProductName,
i.CurrentStock,
i.ReorderLevel
FROM Products p
INNER JOIN Inventory i
ON p.ProductID = i.ProductID;

Concept Learned:

INNER JOIN

Business Question:

Which products require reordering?

Query:

SELECT
ProductID,
CurrentStock,
ReorderLevel,
CASE
WHEN CurrentStock < ReorderLevel
THEN 'Reorder Needed'
ELSE 'Stock Available'
END AS StockStatus
FROM Inventory;

Concept Learned:

CASE WHEN

Business Question:

Which products are below reorder level?

Query:

SELECT
p.ProductName,
i.CurrentStock,
i.ReorderLevel
FROM Products p
JOIN Inventory i
ON p.ProductID = i.ProductID
WHERE i.CurrentStock < i.ReorderLevel;

Concept Learned:

JOIN + WHERE

Business Question:

How many units should be ordered?

Query:

SELECT
ProductID,
CurrentStock,
ReorderLevel,
ReorderLevel - CurrentStock AS ReorderQuantity
FROM Inventory
WHERE CurrentStock < ReorderLevel;

Concept Learned:

Calculated Columns

Business Question:

Which shipments arrived late?

Query:

SELECT
PO_ID,
ProductID,
ExpectedDate,
ReceivedDate
FROM PurchaseOrders
WHERE ReceivedDate > ExpectedDate;

Concept Learned:

Date Comparison

Business Question:

Which suppliers are delivering late?

Query:

SELECT
s.SupplierName,
COUNT(*) AS TotalOrders,
AVG(DATEDIFF(ReceivedDate, ExpectedDate))
AS AverageDelayDays
FROM PurchaseOrders po
JOIN Suppliers s
ON po.SupplierID = s.SupplierID
GROUP BY s.SupplierName;

Concept Learned:

GROUP BY
COUNT
AVG
DATEDIFF

Business Question:

Which products have excess inventory?

Query:

SELECT
ProductID,
CurrentStock,
ReorderLevel
FROM Inventory
WHERE CurrentStock > ReorderLevel * 2;

Concept Learned:

Business Rules

Business Question:

Which products have stock below average?

Query:

SELECT *
FROM Inventory
WHERE CurrentStock <
(
SELECT AVG(CurrentStock)
FROM Inventory
);
